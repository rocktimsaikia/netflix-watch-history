# Privacy Policy

**Watch History for Netflix** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

- The extension runs only on `netflix.com`.
- Thumbnail image URLs are cached in your browser's local storage, on your device only.
- No analytics, no tracking, no external servers.
- No personal information ever leaves your browser.

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/rocktimsaikia/netflix-watch-history/issues
